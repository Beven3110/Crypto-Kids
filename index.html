Crypto Kids

Puzzle 1: The Ancient Scroll

You’ve discovered an ancient scroll with a mysterious message. The message is encoded using the Caesar Cipher, where each letter in the message has been shifted by 5 places in the alphabet. Your task is to decode it and uncover the ancient wisdom.

Encoded Message: MJUJ WTAJ

Puzzle 2: The Pit of Sand

You fall into a pit of sand. As you look around, you see a series of symbols on the wall. Each symbol represents a letter. You must decode the symbols using a simple substitution cipher where A=1, B=2, C=3, etc.

Encoded Symbols: 20-5-19-20 13-1-16

Puzzle 3: The Secret Room

In the secret room, you find a code written in binary. You need to decode it to proceed.

Binary Code: 01010100 01101000 01100101 00100000 01110110 01110101 01101100 01110100

Puzzle 4: The Vault Lock

The vault has a 4-digit combination lock. The combination is derived from a riddle: "My first is in 4 but not in 8, my second is in 7 but not in 3, my third is in 2 but not in 1, my last is in 9 but not in 6."

Puzzle 5: The Ancient Keys

Each key has a number engraved on it. You find a riddle next to the keys: "I am the smallest and the largest, the even and the odd, the start and the end. What number am I?"

Puzzle 6: The Secret Passage

In the passage, you find a set of symbols arranged in a grid. You need to use a simple substitution cipher where each symbol corresponds to a letter of the alphabet.

Encoded Symbols: # @ ! * % & $

Puzzle 7: The Hidden Door

The door is locked with a keypad. The code to unlock it is based on the Fibonacci sequence. The last three digits are needed.

Puzzle 8: The Library

In the library, you find a book with pages marked in Morse code. Decode the Morse code to find the next clue.

Morse Code: ...- ..- -..- --. ..- ..-.

Puzzle 9: The Compass

The compass has letters on it. The direction to go is indicated by a series of coordinates. You need to decode the coordinates using a Vigenère cipher with the keyword "MAP".

Encoded Coordinates: LBGZ FV UMBK

Puzzle 10: The Hidden Chest

The chest has a complex lock. To open it, you need to solve a riddle: "I am not alive, but I can grow; I don’t have lungs, but I need air; I don’t have a mouth, but water kills me. What am I?"
